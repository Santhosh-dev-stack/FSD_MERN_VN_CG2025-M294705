Top 5 Favourite Movies

  1. The Shawshank Redemption (1994)
    • Powerful story of hope and resilience.
    • Memorable performances and strong character development.
    • Emotionally satisfying ending and excellent pacing.
  2. The Godfather (1972)
    • Masterful direction and atmosphere.
    • Complex, well-written characters and family drama.
    • Iconic scenes and unforgettable dialogue.
  3. Inception (2010)
    • Ambitious, layered storytelling with smart world-building.
    • Inventive visual effects and striking set pieces.
    • Thought-provoking themes about memory and reality.
  4. The Dark Knight (2008)
    • Outstanding antagonist performance and moral complexity.
    • High-quality action blended with strong drama.
    • Excellent cinematography and memorable score.
  5. Spirited Away (2001)
    • Rich, imaginative world and breathtaking animation.
    • Beautifully paced coming-of-age story.
    • Deeply symbolic themes and unique visual storytelling.
